Meet Tea Guru

Tea Guru is a ready-to-enjoy tea beverage brewed with intention. Each bottle is balanced to be refreshing, gently flavorful, and easy to drink on its own-no extra sugar bombs, energy spikes, or artificial aftertastes. Just a clean, focused experience you can come back to again and again.

Balanced flavor

Smooth, tea-forward taste with subtle natural notes. Light enough to sip all day, distinct enough to feel like a ritual every time you open a bottle.

Thoughtful ingredients

Tea Guru focuses on ingredients you can recognize and pronounce. Each element is chosen to support a clear, enjoyable drinking experience-nothing loud, nothing random, nothing extra just for the label.

Made for everyday use

Keep a bottle at your desk, in your bag, or in your fridge. Tea Guru is designed to be part of your everyday flow, not just a once-in-a-while special treat.

What's inside the bottle

We believe clarity starts with knowing what you're actually drinking. Tea Guru is built around real tea, clean flavors, and a straightforward approach to what goes on the label.

Key ingredients

  • Brewed tea base - The heart of Tea Guru. Smooth and balanced, not overly bitter or syrupy.
  • Natural flavor notes - Subtle layers that round out the taste without overpowering the tea itself.
  • Citric acid - A touch of brightness to keep things crisp and refreshing.
  • Light sweetness (optional) - If the formulation includes sweetness, it stays gentle and balanced. If unsweetened, the copy can be updated to highlight that instead.

Make it a ritual

Tea Guru isn't about perfection or performing some elaborate ceremony. It's about taking small, intentional pauses in a noisy day-and giving those moments a flavor you actually look forward to.

Morning grounding

Start your day with a few quiet sips before the scroll, the emails, and the rush. Take a breath, pick one intention for the day, and let the rest wait a minute.

Deep work companion

Keep a bottle nearby when you're in focus mode. Reach for it between tasks or during creative blocks to reset your attention and reconnect with why you're doing the work in the first place.

Evening reset

Use Tea Guru as a soft signal that you're shifting gears-closing the laptop, stepping away from the noise, or catching up with someone you care about.

The story behind Tea Guru

Tea Guru started as a conversation between friends who loved tea, loved making things, and were a little bit obsessed with the idea of everyday rituals. We kept coming back to the same question: what if there was a drink that felt intentional without feeling complicated, dramatic, or guilty?

Instead of chasing the loudest energy drink trend or another sugar-loaded bottle, the idea was to build something quieter. A tea beverage that could sit calmly on your desk or in your fridge and still feel special when you cracked it open.

Tea Guru is our answer to that question-a small, focused brand built around clarity, craft, and the belief that little daily choices can change how you feel in a big way.

Built by people who genuinely drink this stuff, tweak it, and care about how it fits into real lives-not just photoshoots.
